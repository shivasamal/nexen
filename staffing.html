Staffing

Tax Consultants/Advisors

These professionals can assist businesses and individuals in making tax-efficient decisions. They provide strategic advice on tax planning, compliance, and minimizing tax liabilities.

Administrative Assistants

In a busy tax office, administrative support staff are crucial for scheduling, organizing client meetings, maintaining records, and managing correspondence.

Tax Managers/Supervisors

These individuals can oversee a team of tax preparers and accountants, ensuring that all filings are accurate and comply with the relevant tax laws.

Client Relationship Managers

These professionals ensure that clients receive the highest level of service and communicate effectively. In a tax company, relationship managers should understand tax laws well enough to communicate with clients about their needs and help build long-term client relationships.
